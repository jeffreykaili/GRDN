About Us

At GRDN, we aspire to create healthier communities by promoting sustainable food practices. We see countless benefits in growing your own food and shopping for local produce, and we are here to share those advantages with you!

  • GRDN provides you with precise guides on how to grow your own food, the equipment required, pictures to guide you along the way, and recipes you can make with the food you grow.
  • Sometimes we still need to go out and shop for our produce, but GRDN provides you with local farmers markets near you to shop at, so you can always be doing your part for the environment.
  • GRDN has been and always will be free and open source. Our only goal is to spread the benefits of home gardening and local produce.

As we are coming out of this pandemic, we have a choice to make. We could go back to the way things were, consuming unnecessary amounts of products that caused tremendous amounts of harm to our environment, or we can make a difference. A difference that can fix the damage we have made, and lead everyone towards a happier, healthier life. GRDN aims to be the start to this change. Tap below to get started!

WHY PLANT WITH GRDN?

Home gardening has been shown to have many health benefits, both mental and physical. Not only is home gardening fun and good for your diet, but it also has a positive impact on the environment. By eating food locally, communities become more sustainable, avoiding the costs that come with transportation and packaging.
